We are Skaaltec


What we stand for:


The company name “Skaaltec” combines a cheerful wish (in Scandinavian) for good health with the technology.
This is exactly the mission of Skaaltec - to provide advanced neuroscience-inspired technology solutions for improved health in an accessible way for everyone.
















The Problem




Stroke is the leading cause of disability globally, without over 100 million survivors who struggle with everyday movements due to brain neural circuit damage. Physical therapy, which is the current standard of stroke recovery, doesn't directly address the core issue - the injured brain motor pathways, and it's primarily limited to short clinic stays. The available tools in the clinics are expensive, complex, are difficult into setup and integrate into clinical workflows. Situation is even worse after the patients are released home - currently, there are no advanced tools available for neural rehabilitation at home at all.









Our Solution







We seek to tackle all of these problems of neurorehabilitation head on and in a single solution - SmartVNS. It appears much as a common smartwatch and an earpiece, and may even be difficult to notice. Despite this SmartVNS is in fact a cutting edge brain-targeted rehabilitation system. It's able to continuously track patient's movement and direct stimulation of an important Vagus nerve with just small electric pulses and without any surgery. All of this packed into a small, easy to use and affordable system.
