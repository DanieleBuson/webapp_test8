Skaaltec Portals



In today's fast-paced world, data is the lifeblood of businesses, driving informed decision-making and innovation. Skaaltec, at the forefront of technological advancement, is proud to present a groundbreaking solution that harnesses the power of data from sensors, seamlessly integrating it through cloud technology, and delivering actionable insights through our powerful portals.


Data from sensors is the hidden treasure trove that holds invaluable information about your operations, processes, and assets. However, without the right tools and infrastructure, harnessing this data can be a daunting challenge. Skaaltec simplifies this process by offering a comprehensive solution that streamlines data acquisition from healthcare sensors, like SmartVNS.



Portal architecture


At the core of our portal system lies a streamlined data architecture designed to efficiently collect, process, and store healthcare information. This architecture seamlessly integrates sensor data into the cloud, ensuring secure transmission and easy access for patients and therapists. Patient data, including medical records and session details, is organized and safeguarded within this architecture. The structured and secure data framework enables the portal system to deliver real-time insights, fostering informed decision-making and personalized care. With our robust data architecture, we ensure that the portals act as a secure gateway to empower both patients and therapists, revolutionizing the healthcare experience.

Patient Portal


Our portal system serves as the central hub for patients and therapists, offering a seamless interface for accessing critical information and enhancing healthcare experiences. Patients gain convenient access to their personal health data, including medical records and treatment progress. This transparency empowers patients to make informed decisions and actively engage in their care.

Therapist Portal


For therapists, these portals provide a comprehensive view of patient history and real-time data from sessions. This data-driven approach enables therapists to tailor treatment strategies, monitor outcomes, and maintain secure communication with patients between sessions. Ultimately, our portals foster better patient outcomes by facilitating personalized care and improved decision-making, making them a cornerstone of modern healthcare architecture.
